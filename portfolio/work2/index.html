Educate-An online education platform

The COVID-19 has had a big impact on higher education. We designed an online education platform that help collage students transit to online education and keep a high study performance when they have to maintain social distance.

Five steps of the design thinking process were gone through to improve the online studying experience of college students in higher education. These steps are in order:

  • empathize
  • define
  • ideate
  • prototype
  • test 　　

　 　

01

EMPATHIZE

Target group: college students.

Interview

Eight semi-structured interviews were operated within our target group to get a better understanding of their needs. 　

The first and second parts focused on some general information about the background and demographics of the interviewee and are as such structured. The third and fourth part are about social needs and online education.

　 　 　

02

DEFINE

For the interview results, the thematic analysis is done using Affinity diagram. Four main themes were discovered.

After the analysis, “Online learning” was defined as the biggest problem and our platform would be focus on online education based on the analysis result.

03

Ideation

Brainstorming

Using opppsite thinking method.

Define the solution

Using COCD box method.

Ethical Envisioning

Criteria of stakeholders and pervasiveness were used.

04

Prototype

Low-fidelity prototyping

05

Test

  • Pilot experiment
  • THINK OUT LOUD method
  • Categorized their insights and answers
