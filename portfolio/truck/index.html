Automotive system engineering project - Design an autonomous truck

A digital twin autonomous truck system(DTADTS) was created.

The context is the trucks move autonomously within the distribution center(DC) loading goods and is manually drived before entering the DC. All aspects of the truck system were considered, including the powertrain, the vehicle dynamics, the communication channel, the human factors and the functional safety. The model driven system engineering method was mainly used in this project, which connected different domains of this project together. I was in charge of the automotive human factor domain. The human machine interface(HMI) was designed for the system and the external HMI(eHMI) was developed for the truck.
